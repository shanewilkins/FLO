# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.
We aim to build a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in communication.
- Assume good intent and seek clarification before escalating.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith engagement.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to project maintainers.
For potential security-related concerns, follow `SECURITY.md`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled in good faith and as confidentially as practical.
